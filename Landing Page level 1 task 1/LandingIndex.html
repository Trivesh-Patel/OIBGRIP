Our Services

Pec Deck Machine

This particular machine is designed, similarly like chest press machine, to isolate chest muscles, specifically pectoral muscles. The machine is used in a seated position with your elbows at 90 degrees and the point is to use the two mechanical arms to bring them together in front of your face.

45-Degree leg press

Leg press machine can work in two separate, yet similar ways. Both start in the sitting position (either flat or incline), then you are either pushing weight stacks with your legs in front of you, or you are pulling yourself away from that weight stack.

Treadmill

It's basically a piece of machine designed for running in the same space via the use of a rotating belt. This actually dates back centuries to standard mills, which were manually operated by animals or humans.

Leg-Curl

This workout is all about isolating the quadriceps, which are the large muscles at the front side of the thighs. Very few machines are able to target and isolate the quads like this one.

Squat Machine

A hack squat works the entire lower body, including the glutes, hamstrings, quads, and calves, as well as the core muscles. The main emphasis is on the quads, so expect that the front of your legs will be feeling it the next day.

Multiple Weights

There's no limit to the muscles you can work while using a set of weight plates. More often than not, they're used for working the upper body muscles, but they can also be used for core and lower body workouts.
